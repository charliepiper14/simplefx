What Module 1 Covers

A full day designed to take you from zero understanding to a clear, working knowledge of how forex actually operates.

1

The Language Of The Market

Pips, lots, spreads, leverage, and currency pairs explained properly. The vocabulary every trader needs before anything else makes sense.

Reading A Chart Like A Trader

Candlesticks, timeframes, and what price action is actually telling you. Watch real charts read live by your mentor.
